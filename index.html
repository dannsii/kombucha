About Cazalu Kombucha

Kombucha is a fermented, slightly alcoholic beverage made from sweetened tea and a SCOBY (Symbiotic Culture of Bacteria and Yeast). According to the FDA, most people can safely consume up to four ounces of kombucha per day.

Our Fruity Kombucha merges the natural fizz of fermented tea with the bright flavors of real fruit juices. Featuring a blend of cranberry, mixed berries, and grapefruit, it offers a modern, refreshing twist that appeals to Gen Z, millennials, and older health-conscious consumers alike.

Brewed in small batches and sustainably packaged, Fruity Kombucha is a vibrant, probiotic-rich choice for those seeking both flavor and wellness benefits.
